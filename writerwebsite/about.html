About Emma

Emma Smith-Stevens grew up in New York City. She has worked as a server at a pancake house in Delray Beach, Florida, a gift-wrapper in Boca Raton, a personal assistant in Los Angeles, and an adjunct professor at the University of Florida and Santa Fe College. Now living in Gainesville, she is a scriptwriter for virtual patients used by nursing students, and is completing her first novel and short story collection.

Her fiction has been published in Conjunctions, Subtropics, The Collagist, PANK, Painted Bride Quarterly, and elsewhere, and was included on Wigleaf 's Top 50 (Very) Short Fictions list in 2012 and 2013. She is a graduate of Bard College and the University of Florida's MFA program in creative writing.
